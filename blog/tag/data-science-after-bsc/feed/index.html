Data Science After BSc -FAQs

Q1. Can I be a data scientist after my BSc?

Ans. After earning a BSc degree, pursuing a career in data science is promising. Data scientists use machine learning and advanced programming to create innovative algorithms and automate tasks.

Q2. What is the monthly income of a data scientist?

Ans. The average salary for a Data Scientist in India is ₹14.4 lakhs per year (equivalent to ₹1.2 lakhs per month). These salary estimates are derived from data collected from 38.4k recent salaries of Data Scientists across various industries.

Q3. What is the minimum salary of a data scientist in India?

Ans. The starting salary for a Data Scientist is INR 4.96 LPA, while the highest salary range typically falls between INR 37 LPA to 50 LPA.
