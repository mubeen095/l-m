Data Science Course Syllabus

This Data Science Course will teach students the ways to work with structured and unstructured data. This can be done with the help of some tools, algorithms, and software. Therefore, students should check the data science syllabus before choosing a university to enroll.

We know that data science courses include subjects like Statistics, Programming, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Mathematics, and Data Mining. Moreover, these subjects can be seen in every data science course, starting from the online, classroom-based, and full-time degrees.

What is Data Science and its Syllabus?

Data Science is a discipline that involves the study of data to extract meaningful insights of data. Data Science has become the fastest-evolving field in the IT Sector. Thus, the topics that will come under Data Science are the following:

  1. Artificial Intelligence
  2. Auto Machine Learning
  3. Natural Language Processing (NLP)
  4. Data Fabric
  5. Cloud Migration
  6. Data as a Service
  7. Robotic Process Automation (RPA)
  8. Federated Learning
  9. Data Democratisation
  10. Data Regulation and Governance
