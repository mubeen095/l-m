Graphic Designer Salary

Graphic design is all about creating visual art that communicates messages. This job is important in India and comes with different salaries. Graphic designers make logos, websites, and more to show what a company is like. This guide will help you understand how much money graphic designers can make in India and what things affect their pay. If you’re interested in art and design, this is a helpful read.

Who is Graphic Designer ?

Graphic designers use computers to make special visuals that help create a unique look and feel for a brand. They make things that catch people’s attention and make them interested.

Different companies might want them to do different things, but generally, here are the main jobs of a graphic designer in top companies:

  1. Plan, create, and manage print designs on time
  2. Talk to clients to understand what they need
  3. Work with brand owners to make special designs
  4. Decide on details like font, size, colors, and how things look
  5. Use computer programs to make logos, pictures, and other stuff.

What are the Factors Affecting Graphic Designer Salaries in India?

A graphic designer’s salary isn’t decided by just one thing. Instead, a bunch of things together make up how much they get paid. Here are the main things that decide a graphic designer’s salary:

  1. Experience: The more years a designer works, the more they usually get paid. For example, if they have 5 to 6 years of experience, they might get a raise of ₹10 – 15k for each year.
  2. Education: This is important too. If a graphic designer has a master’s degree or a special certificate in the right subjects, they can ask for more money.
  3. Skills: The skills a designer has also matter. If they’re really good at what they do, they might earn more, especially if they’re senior designers.
  4. Location: Where the company is and where the designer works can also change how much they’re paid.
  5. Performance: If a junior designer does a great job, they could get promoted to a higher position and earn more money as a senior designer.

What are the Different Graphic Designer Job Profiles?

  1. Freelance Graphic Designer: Freelance graphic designers work on different projects as freelancers. They use tools and tech to create images and designs. They can earn around ₹3.0 lacs.
  2. Creative Graphic Designer: These designers plan and make visual stuff using words, images, colors, and more. They make about ₹4.0 lacs.
  3. Motion Graphic Designer: They make artwork for things like movies, commercials, and more. They’re really good at animations and can earn up to ₹4.2 lacs.
  4. Web Designer: Web designers make websites look nice, including logos, fonts, and pictures. They get about ₹3.2 lacs.
  5. Logo Designer: Logo designers make special logos for people and companies. They might work alone or at a company and earn around ₹2.0 lacs.
  6. Senior Graphic Designer: Senior designers get paid more, around ₹5.0 lacs. They handle everything about design, like planning illustrations, infographics, and marketing things like emails and social media posts.

What are the Factors Affecting Graphic Designer Salaries in India?

A single factor cannot determine the salary of a graphic designer. Instead, a combination of factors influences the overall compensation. The following crucial factors contribute to deciding a graphic designer salary. 

  • Years of experience: The years that graphic designers vest in the industry are significant contributors to their salary numbers. For instance, a graphic designer with 5 to 6 years of experience can expect a salary hike of ₹10 -15k per year of experience.   
  • Education: It is the second most crucial factor. Graphic designers who possess a master’s or a certification in relevant subjects find it convenient to negotiate a higher salary.  
  • Skills: The relevant industry skills can be other critical determiners of a senior graphic designer’s salary. 
  • Location: The company and the location have a crucial role in influencing a graphic designer’s salary in India. 
  • Performance: In junior graphic designer positions, better performance can promote an individual to the senior level and raise the salary.

What are the Different Graphic Designer Job Profiles

Various graphic designers’ job profiles are available depending on the candidate’s skills and experience level. 

1. Freelance Graphic Designer 

A freelance graphic designer takes up designing projects on a freelance basis. Several popular freelancing websites provide multiple job opportunities for freelancers, such as Freelancer.com, Upwork, and Fiverr. A freelance graphic designer assembles images and graphics creatively while employing tools and technology. The average freelance graphic designer salary in India is ₹3.0 lacs. 

2. Creative Graphic Designer 

A creative graphic designer plans and projects visual communication via imagery, words, space, fonts, and color palettes. The average salary of a creative graphic designer is ₹4.0 lacs. 

3. Motion Graphic Designer

In India, a motion graphic designer can make up to ₹4.2 lacs per annum. They are responsible for creating web, television, or film artwork, such as movie clips, trailers, commercials, title sequences, etc. They master animation and other cinematic techniques that add life to their creations.

4. Web Designer 

The average salary of a web designer is ₹3.2 lacs per annum. There is a high demand for these professionals who are masterminds behind every aesthetically pleasing website. They curate the aesthetics of a website, including logos, fonts, and images. A graphic web designer collaborates with other designers and web developers to finalize a direction for the website’s image.

5. Logo Designer

These professional graphic designers create unique and customized brand logos for the individual or company they work for. They can be freelancers or full-time employees at an advertising agency or design firm. According to the research, they have an average annual salary of ₹2.0 lacs.

6. Senior Graphic Designer

The senior positions offer higher salaries concerning a candidate’s expertise. India’s average senior graphic designer salary is ₹5.0 lacs per annum. Senior designers are responsible for all aspects of graphic design. They manage planning out illustrations, infographics, and marketing materials such as emails, social media posts, etc.

FAQ- Graphic Designer Salary

Q1. What is the highest salary for a Graphic Designer in India?

Ans. Senior Graphic Designer salary in India ranges between ₹ 2.5 Lakhs to ₹ 10.3 Lakhs with an average annual salary of ₹ 5.0 Lakhs.

Q2. Is graphic design stressful?

Ans. Being a graphic designer can sometimes be stressful, especially when there’s not much time, lots of projects to handle, or clients who are hard to work with. But, you can manage stress by handling time well, talking well with others, and taking care of yourself. These things can help you deal with the tough parts of the job.

Q3. Is graphic design a worthy career?

Ans. If you want to do something creative, being a graphic designer lets you use your artistic skills in different ways. You could work from home, in an office, or even as a freelancer. As a graphic designer, you get to show your creativity and passion for art.
