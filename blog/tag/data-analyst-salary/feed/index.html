Data Analyst Salary

In 2023, data analysts are in high demand as they help organizations make decisions based on data. This article will discuss how much data analysts earn this year. We’ll look at what factors affect their salaries and what opportunities exist in this career. Whether you’re starting out or experienced, knowing what data analysts can expect to earn in 2023 is valuable.

Who is a Data Analyst

A data analyst’s job is to gather, tidy up, and make sense of data to find answers or solve problems. They work in various fields like business, finance, law enforcement, science, healthcare, and government.

Types of Data Analysts

In today’s tech-driven world, the ability to collect, organize, and analyze data has become essential across various industries. Data analysts are now integral in fields like criminal justice, fashion, food, technology, business, the environment, and the public sector, among many more.

Professionals who specialize in data analysis can go by different titles, such as:

  • Medical and healthcare analyst
  • Market research analyst
  • Business analyst
  • Business intelligence analyst
  • Operations research analyst

These roles all involve the important task of making sense of data to inform decision-making in their respective fields.

Average Data Analyst Salary

The average Data Analyst Salary in India is INR 5.50 LPA. According to Glassdoor, entry-level Data Analysts can expect a starting salary ranging from INR 2.51 LPA to INR 4.80 LPA. The salary of Data Analysts depends on several critical factors such as experience, skills, and qualifications. Data Analysts with 5-7 years of experience can earn an average salary of INR 6.80 LPA. Those with an undergraduate degree earn around INR 3.25 LPA, while those with a postgraduate degree earn approximately INR 10.3 LPA.

Some of the top recruiters for Data Analysts include IBM, Accenture, Wipro, Microsoft, Capgemini, and Cognizant. Google offers the highest salaries in the market for Data Analysts, with an average package of INR 13.40 LPA. The highest Data Analyst Salary can go as high as INR 22 LPA for those with exceptional skills and experience.

FAQ- Data Analyst Salary In India 2023

Q1. Is data analyst high salary?

Ans.Data Analysts in India earn an average annual salary of ₹4.2 Lakhs, with salaries ranging from ₹1.7 Lakhs to ₹11.5 Lakhs. Salary levels depend on factors like experience and skills.

Q2.What is the salary of a 2 year data analyst in India?

Ans. For Data Analysts in India with 2 years or less to 7 years of experience, salaries can range from ₹3.3 Lakhs to ₹24.0 Lakhs, with an average annual salary of ₹10.0 Lakhs

Q3. Is data analyst a lot of coding?

Ans. Certainly, coding is essential for an online Data Analytics Degree. It’s necessary for data manipulation and analysis, particularly with languages like Python or R. Proficiency in coding is vital for effective data analysis and is often a requirement in data analytics roles.
