When Identifying Processes To Bring Into Salesforce Look For

Answer. Option E ) is the Correct Option

All of the options (A, B, C, and D) can be good candidates for improvement or automation using Salesforce or similar CRM (Customer Relationship Management) and business process management tools. These scenarios often involve inefficiencies and can benefit from streamlining automation, and centralized data management, which Salesforce can provide.
