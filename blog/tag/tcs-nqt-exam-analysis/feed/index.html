TCS NQT Exam Analysis – FAQs

Q1.What is a good score in TCS NQT?

Ans. There is no fixed minimum percentage to clear the TCS NQT exam – it depends on the number of candidates appearing. However, candidates should aim to score at least 60% across different sections to have a good chance of qualifying for the next round.

Q2.How is the TCS NQT score calculated?

Ans.The difficulty level of the exam paper and how candidates performed in it are analyzed by TCS.

Q3.Is the TCS NQT exam tough?

Ans. The TCS NQT exam is moderate to difficult, the quantitative aptitude section being the toughest part. To do well, candidates should practice, especially by solving the previous year’s NQT question papers from TCS.

Q4.Is it easy to pass TCS NQT?

Ans. The TCS NQT exam has a moderate to high difficulty level, so candidates have a strong base of technical and non-technical skills. The syllabus covers topics like Quantitative Aptitude, Logical Reasoning, Verbal Ability, and Programming Concepts, requiring good preparation across multiple fields.

Q5.Is 70 percent good in TCS NQT?

Ans. Getting a score – 1276 out of 1800 in the TCS NQT exam is considered a good score. With this score, you need to receive an interview call from TCS.

Q6.Is 60% required for TCS Nqt?

Ans. For eligibility, you need a minimum of 60% marks or 6.5 CGPA in your graduation degree. The eligible streams are BE/B. Tech, MCA, and M.Sc, and up to 1 active backlog is allowed.

Q7.Can I skip the TCS NQT exam?

Ans. Attempting the advanced section is not compulsory if you apply for the TCS Ninja profile. However, to be considered for the TCS Digital and Prime profiles, you must attempt the advanced section and score well .

Q8.How to crack TCS aptitude?

Ans.Practice solving different types of reasoning puzzles to improve your problem-solving abilities. Ensure you are proficient in the required programming language and have thoroughly covered all important topics from the syllabus. Regularly practicing numerical problems will help increase your speed and accuracy.
