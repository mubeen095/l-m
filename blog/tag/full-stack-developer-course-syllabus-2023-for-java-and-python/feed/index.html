FAQ – Full Stack Developer Course Syllabus

Q1. Is Java Full Stack Developer good career in 2024?

Ans. To sum up, a career as a Java Full Stack Developer is both thrilling and demanding, presenting numerous advantages such as strong demand, attractive salaries, and a diverse array of job prospects.

Q2. Which Full Stack Developer demand in 2024?

Ans. By 2024, full-stack development is anticipated to shift towards microservices, serverless architecture, and containerization. Concurrently, developers are exploring novel frontend frameworks and libraries like React, Vue.js, and Angular, alongside sophisticated backend technologies such as GraphQL and Kubernetes.

Q3. What is the salary of full stack developer in India 2023?

Ans. In India, the typical salary for a full stack developer averages around ₹375,000. For those with 1-4 years of experience, the annual salary stands at approximately ₹553,006. Meanwhile, mid-level professionals with 5-9 years of expertise can expect an average salary ranging from 12 to 14 lakhs.
