IIBA has divided countries into three regions. Region 1 includes countries like the USA, Australia, Canada, UK, UAE, etc. Region 2 includes countries like Singapore, Taiwan, South Africa, etc. Region 3 includes countries like India, Pakistan, Argentina, etc.

Professionals from wealthier countries in Region 1 pay more for the certification, while those from economically weaker countries in Region 3 pay less. If you’re in India, your ECBA certification cost breakdown would be as follows:
