Infosys Exam Preparation Tips for 2024

The 5 Preparation tips for the Infosys Examination are added here. Graduates can check out the Infosys Exam Preparation Tip below:

1. Understand the Question Paper Structure

The online test lasts for 1 hour 45 minutes. It consists of sections, such as verbal, pseudo-code, and technical sections.

There is a sectional cut-off, but no negative marking for incorrect answers. You must complete one section at a time and cannot switch between sections. Your answers are automatically saved once the time for each section is up. If you complete a section before the allotted time, go to the next section. Attempt all questions, and for any unsolved or unknown questions, choose the same answer option consistently.

3. Solve Infosys Previous Year Paper

Solving previous year’s papers is crucial for your preparation. Start by understanding the exam pattern, section-wise weightage, and question types. Analyze your strong and weak areas from the previous year’s papers. For difficult questions, read the explanations thoroughly to grasp the concepts.

Practice similar questions repeatedly until you are confident. Time management is key – refer to the sectional time limits and practice accordingly.

Additionally, take mock tests to simulate the real exam environment and improve your speed and accuracy. Consistently practicing previous year’s papers will boost your confidence and help you identify your blind spots to work on.

4. Start with Easy Questions First

Some questions are challenging and require deep understanding, while others are easier to solve with practice. Within the first 15 minutes, focus on attempting 6 less time-consuming questions, including 3 to 4 easy ones that can be solved quickly. Then, spend more time on the remaining 4 questions.

The goal is to get at least 6 to 7 correct answers to meet the expected cut-off. The most crucial part is identifying the 6 less time-consuming questions and solving them first.

Graduates can begin with questions that can be solved quickly. Get through 9 of those in 15 minutes. Then do the Data Interpretation questions that use charts/tables. Leave the Data Arrangement questions until the end since they are more time-consuming. You can get 10-11 questions right overall, you can meet the cut-off requirement.

5. Try the Error Spotting or Sentence Correction Next

Start with the easier questions like Sentence Correction, Error Spotting, and Vocabulary. Then, move to Reading Comprehension, tackling longer passages first but not spending too much time understanding every detail.

Answer the straightforward questions based on the passage within 5 minutes, and make guesses for the remaining questions.

Finally, attempt 5-6 Critical Reasoning questions within 10 minutes. The goal is to get at least 20 correct answers to meet the expected cut-off score. Remember, there’s no negative marking, so it’s better to attempt all questions instead of leaving any blank.

Important Things to Remember for Infosys Exam Preparation

  • Infosys generally has a 65% cut-off mark for any test during training programs.
  • For the recruitment test, you can expect a similar 65% cut-off for Sections I and II.
  • However, the actual cut-off mark for your college or university is different.

Infosys decides this cut-off based on a few factors:

  1. How well did students from your college/university perform in the past when they got selected at Infosys?
  2. How the alumni (former students) from your college/university are currently performing at Infosys.
  3. The percentage of students from your college/university who left Infosys after completing the training.
