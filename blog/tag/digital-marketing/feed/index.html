What is Digital Marketing?

To promote a product or service, digital marketing uses digital channels, such as search engines, social media, emails, and websites. It involves various strategies, such as search engine optimization (SEO), pay-per-click advertising (PPC), content marketing, social media marketing, email marketing, and mobile marketing.

Why is Digital Marketing Important?

In today’s digital age, most people spend a significant amount of time online, and businesses must establish a strong online presence to reach their target audience. Digital marketing enables businesses to connect with their customers and target them more effectively. It is also cost-effective compared to traditional marketing methods, and it provides businesses with measurable results.
