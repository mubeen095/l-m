Which Of The Following Would Be An Ideal Goal For A Seo Plan?

Ans. Option B) Increasing how many relevant people visit your website is the Correct Answer

An ideal goal for an SEO (Search Engine Optimization) plan depends on the specific objectives and needs of the website or online business. This goal focuses on improving the website’s presence in search engine results pages (SERPs) and achieving higher rankings for relevant keywords.
