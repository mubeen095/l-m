What is the TCS BPS Job In-hand Salary 2024?

As a fresher joining TCS BPS, you can earn between Rs. 15,000 – Rs. 23,000 monthly. However, the actual amount you receive in hand may vary. This is because your gross salary, before any deductions, is subject to deductions like Provident Fund, Employee State Insurance (ESI), Professional Tax, and others. These deductions are taken from your salary to fulfill legal requirements and other purposes. So, your take-home pay, or in-hand salary, will be the amount left after these deductions. Make sure to understand these deductions to know how much you’ll receive.

TCS BPS Job and their Additional Benefits

TCS BPS (Tata Consultancy Services Business Process Services) offers a comprehensive benefits package to its employees in addition to their monthly salary of Rs. 23,000. The package includes a health insurance plan that provides coverage for medical expenses, although the plan’s specific details are not mentioned. Employees contribute Rs. 804 per month to a provident fund, a retirement savings scheme where the company also makes contributions.

The total annual contribution to the provident fund amounts to Rs. 9,648. TCS BPS also has a gratuity fund, a lump sum payment made to employees upon retirement or termination of employment. The employee’s monthly contribution to the gratuity fund is Rs. 322, amounting to Rs. 3,867 annually. The total monthly contribution towards these components and retirement benefits is Rs. 1,126, and the annual contribution amounts to Rs. 21,415. With a monthly salary of Rs. 23,000 and these additional benefits, TCS BPS employees receive a compensation package.
