What is the TCS BPS Syllabus?

The TCS BPS Online Test comprises 50 questions across different subjects including Numerical Ability, Verbal Ability, Reasoning Ability, Quantitative Aptitude, and Data Interpretation. Negative marking is not applied in the TCS BPS test, meaning candidates won’t lose marks for wrong answers.

Q2.Where can I find the TCS BPS Syllabus?

Ans. You can find the TCS BPS Syllabus on the official TCS website or through the link provided in the job application portal.

Q3.Is the TCS BPS Syllabus the same every year?

Ans. While the core subjects remain consistent, slight variations may occur in the syllabus yearly. It’s essential to refer to the most recent syllabus for accurate preparation.

Q4.How should I prepare for the TCS BPS Syllabus?

Ans. To prepare for the TCS BPS Syllabus, focus on understanding the key concepts and practicing with relevant study materials, mock tests, and previous years’ question papers.

Q5.Are there any specific resources recommended for TCS BPS Syllabus preparation?

Ans.TCS provides study materials and practice tests on its official website. Several online platforms offer study materials and practice tests tailored for TCS BPS.

Q6.Is there a negative marking in the TCS BPS Online Test?

Ans.No, there is no negative marking in the TCS BPS Online Test. Candidates won’t lose marks for incorrect answers.

Q7.How many questions are in each section of the TCS BPS Online Test?

Ans. The number of questions varies for each section. Generally, Numerical Ability, Verbal Ability, and Reasoning Ability have around 10-12 questions each, while Quantitative Aptitude and Data Interpretation have fewer questions.

Q8.Can I expect questions beyond the specified syllabus in the TCS BPS Online Test?

Ans. It’s unlikely, as the test is designed to assess candidates based on the specified syllabus. However, candidates should be prepared for different question types within the topics.
